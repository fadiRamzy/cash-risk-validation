## Important note on scope and integrity

This is genuinely an enterprise-scale reference project — realistically 100+ pages across ~14 modules. I'm building it **section by section, with real sourcing**, rather than generating it all in one pass, for two reasons:

1. **Your own instructions require sourcing every rule** ("never invent policies/laws/criteria"). To do that honestly I need to pull and verify each rule from FRA publications, the Law 141/2014 text and amendments, and (where no Egyptian source exists) clearly-labeled CGAP/World Bank/IFC/AFI material. That's genuine research per section, not something I should fabricate to move faster.
2. **Public web access to FRA's actual PDF circulars/inspection manuals is limited** — many are only published as scanned Arabic PDFs on fra.gov.eg without stable indexable text. Where I cannot verify a specific rule from a citable source, I will explicitly mark it `[UNVERIFIED — confirm with FRA circular / internal compliance]` rather than invent it. This matches your "never invent" rule.

I'll keep working section-by-section in this conversation. Tell me if you want to reorder priorities (e.g. Observation Library + Business Activities first since those are what you'll use daily, vs. Legal Framework which is reference material).
